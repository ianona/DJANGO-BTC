Welcome

Bitcoin is famous for being one of the world's first cryptocurrency and online payment system. The development of a cryptocurrency was done so that people may own cash digitally for the convenience of being able to buy and avail products and services easily online. It was the first decentralized currency as there was no central bank or administrator controlling the bitcoin system and was digital making it a hot topic at its time. Bitcoin was invented by an unknown group of people or persons under the name of Satoshi Nakamoto and was released as an open-source software in 2009.
